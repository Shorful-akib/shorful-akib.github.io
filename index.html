Biography

I am a statistician from Bangladesh with an MSc in Statistics from Shahjalal University of Science and Technology (SUST). My research centers on Bayesian methodology — particularly empirical Bayes estimation — applied to health and demographic data, including a first-author manuscript estimating adult mortality from Demographic and Health Survey (DHS) sibling histories. I am seeking a fully funded PhD in Statistics, Biostatistics, or a related quantitative field.
